My swirl

Description

Ingredients

Ice Cream Base

  • Choose your favorite ice cream flavor (e.g., vanilla, chocolate, strawberry, etc.).

Toppings

  • American Cookies
  • Oreo Cookies
  • Muffins (any flavor you prefer)
  • Butterscotch Sauce
  • Cinnamon Mix (optional: sprinkle a mix of cinnamon and sugar on top)

Steps:

  1. Start with a generous swirl of your chosen ice cream flavor as the base.
  2. Add a layer of American Cookies on top of the ice cream.
  3. Crush some Oreo Cookies and sprinkle them over the cookies layer.
  4. Cut the muffins into small pieces and place them on the ice cream.
  5. Drizzle the butterscotch sauce over the entire mixture.
  6. (Optional) If you enjoy the warm and cozy flavor of cinnamon, sprinkle a mix of cinnamon and sugar over the top.
  7. (Optional) For a tangy twist, add a scoop of frozen yogurt on top.
  8. Grab a spoon and enjoy the magical combination of flavors!
Note: Feel free to customize the toppings according to your taste preferences. You can also add some colorful sprinkles or chocolate chips for extra fun!
