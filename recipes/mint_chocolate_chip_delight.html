Mint Chocolate Chip Delight

Description

Ingredients

Ice Cream Base

  • Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream

Toppings

  • Chocolate Syrup
  • Chopped Fresh Mint Leaves
  • Mini Chocolate Chips
  • Whipped Cream

Steps:

  1. Start with a generous scoop of mint chocolate chip ice cream as the base.
  2. Drizzle chocolate syrup over the ice cream.
  3. Sprinkle chopped fresh mint leaves on top for a refreshing taste.
  4. Add a handful of mini chocolate chips for some extra chocolatey goodness.
  5. Finish it off with a dollop of whipped cream.
