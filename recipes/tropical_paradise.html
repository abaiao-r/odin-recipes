Tropical Paradise

Description

Ingredients

Ice Cream Base

  • Coconut Ice Cream

Toppings

  • Pineapple Chunks
  • Mango Slices
  • Toasted Coconut Flakes
  • Passion Fruit Sauce

Steps:

  1. Start with a scoop of creamy coconut ice cream as the base.
  2. Arrange pineapple chunks and mango slices on top of the ice cream.
  3. Sprinkle toasted coconut flakes over the tropical fruits for added texture and flavor.
  4. Drizzle a generous amount of passion fruit sauce over the entire creation.
